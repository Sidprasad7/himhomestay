✨ What to eat in Manali

Manali isn’t just about mountains and views, it’s also a paradise for food lovers. From local Himachali flavors to cozy cafe dishes, the food here adds a special charm to your trip.

Here’s a guide to the must-try food in Manali:

1. Siddu (Local Himachali Dish)

A traditional delicacy, Siddu is a steamed bread made from wheat flour and stuffed with savory fillings. It’s best enjoyed with ghee or chutney and gives you a true taste of Himachal.

2. Dham (Himachali Thali)

A complete traditional meal served with rice, dal, rajma, and local curries. Simple, flavorful, and deeply satisfying—perfect if you want to try authentic local cuisine.

3. Momos & Thukpa

A must-have in the mountains! Hot momos paired with spicy chutney and a bowl of thukpa (noodle soup) are perfect for chilly evenings.

4. Pancakes & Cafe Breakfasts

Manali is known for its cozy cafés. From fluffy pancakes to continental breakfasts, it’s a great place to enjoy slow mornings with good food and mountain views.

5. Fresh Coffee & Bakery Items

Try freshly brewed coffee along with cakes, brownies, and croissants at local cafés—perfect for relaxing and soaking in the vibe.

6. Tibetan & Asian Cuisine

Thanks to its culture, Manali offers delicious Tibetan and Asian dishes like noodles, soups, and stir-fried meals that are both comforting and flavorful.

7. Bonfire Dinner Experience

Enjoy a cozy evening with hot food, warm drinks, and a bonfire under the stars—one of the most memorable experiences in Manali.
